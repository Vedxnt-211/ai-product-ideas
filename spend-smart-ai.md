# AI Smart Expense Tracker
## Problem
Many users struggle to track spending habits consistently.
## Proposed Solution
An AI-based expense management app that:
- categorizes expenses automatically,
- predicts spending patterns,
- suggests savings opportunities,
- and creates monthly financial insights.
## Features
- Smart categorization
- AI spending analysis
- Budget recommendations
- Expense alerts
## Target Users
- Students
- Young professionals
- Freelancers
